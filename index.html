📐 How IQR Winsorization works here:

For each Group × Year combination (e.g. each facility-year), all monthly values (month 1 – 12) form the distribution.
IQR is computed across those monthly observations within that group-year.

  Q1 = 25th percentile of months  |  Q3 = 75th percentile  |  IQR = Q3 − Q1
  Lower Bound = Q1 − 1.5 × IQR  |  Upper Bound = Q3 + 1.5 × IQR

Any monthly value outside [Lower, Upper] → flagged Outlier and clipped to the bound (winsorized).
